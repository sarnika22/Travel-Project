ALAPUZZHA BEACH

Alappuzha (Alleppey) is a landmark between a network of rivers flowing into it and the Arabian Sea. Hallowed with natures green beauty and scenery, Alappuzha is a wonderland of Kerala state. Also, you can find an old lighthouse nearby Alappuzha beach which is very bewitching to visitors.

POTHAMEDU VIEW POINT(munnar)

This spectacular spot is visited by a large number of tourists every year, who get the opportunity to trek through the lush green forest of Munnar, which is only 3 km away from Pothamedu ViewpointThis stunning location is perfect for tourists, who love to view the refreshing plantations of coffee, tea, and cardamom and feel the serenity of the place with their eyes and body drenched in the glory of lush green valleys.

WAYANAD WILDLIFE SANCTUARY

Wayanadin Kerala is famous for its spice plantations and wildlife. Wayanad wildlife reserve is an integral part of the Nilgiri biosphere reserve peacefully located amidst the serene hills of Western Ghats. Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary is the second largest wildlife sanctuary in Kerala and comprises of rare as well as endangered species of both flora and fauna.
